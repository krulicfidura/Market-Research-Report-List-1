<p><p>The tufted carpet tile market comprises three main types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile features individual yarn tufts cut at the top, offering a soft texture and luxurious feel, ideal for comfort-focused areas. Loop pile consists of uncut loops, providing durability and a textured appearance, suitable for high-traffic zones. Cut and loop pile combines both styles, allowing for creative patterns and varying textures, making it versatile for diverse design needs in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
