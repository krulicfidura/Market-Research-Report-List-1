<p><p>Hydraulic Service Manifolds (HSMs) are essential components in hydraulic systems, managing fluid flow and pressure distribution. The market for HSMs can be categorized into two main types: modular manifolds and single-piece manifolds. Modular manifolds offer flexibility, allowing for easy customization and expansion by adding or removing modular sections. In contrast, single-piece manifolds provide simplicity and robustness, as they are constructed from a single block, enhancing reliability in high-pressure applications. Both types cater to varying industry needs.</p></p>
