<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully-Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Case formers are categorized into semi-automatic and fully-automatic types based on their functionality and level of automation. Semi-automatic case formers require some manual intervention, often needing operators to position boxes and initiate the forming process, making them suitable for moderate production rates. In contrast, fully-automatic case formers operate independently, automatically forming and sealing cases without manual input, ideal for high-volume production environments. This automation enhances efficiency and reduces labor costs while maintaining consistent packaging quality.</p></p>
