<p><p>The cat litter market encompasses three main types: clay, silica, and biodegradable litter. Clay cat litter, often made from sodium bentonite, absorbs moisture and controls odors effectively. Silica cat litter uses crystalline silica gel, providing excellent moisture absorption and odor control with minimal dust. Biodegradable cat litter, made from natural materials like corn, wheat, or recycled paper, offers an eco-friendly alternative, breaking down over time and reducing environmental impact. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and concerns.</p></p>
