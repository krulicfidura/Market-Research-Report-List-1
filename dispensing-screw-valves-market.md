<p><p>The Dispensing Screw Valves market comprises two main types: One-Component and Two-Component dispensing screw valves. One-Component dispensing screw valves are designed for single-material applications, allowing precise control of fluid flow for uniform dispensing. In contrast, Two-Component dispensing screw valves are utilized for applications requiring a mixture of two materials, ensuring accurate ratio mixing and simultaneous dispensing. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical, enabling efficient production processes and high-quality results.</p></p>
