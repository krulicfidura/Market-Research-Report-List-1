<p><p>The sterile surgical catgut market encompasses two primary types: plain catgut and chromic catgut. Plain catgut, made from natural fibers, is absorbed by the body over time, making it suitable for internal suturing. Chromic catgut is treated with chromium salt to prolong its absorption period, offering greater durability for surgical applications. Both types are utilized in various surgical procedures, with their selection based on the specific healing requirements and desired duration of support in tissue repair.</p></p>
