<p><strong>The Hollow Fiber Membrane Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrafiltration (UF) Membranes</li><li>Microfiltration (MF) Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow fiber membrane modules are essential components in filtration processes, primarily categorized into ultrafiltration (UF) and microfiltration (MF) membranes. UF membranes have smaller pore sizes, effectively removing macromolecules, colloids, and microorganisms while allowing water and low molecular weight solutes to pass through. In contrast, MF membranes feature larger pores, mainly filtering out suspended solids and larger particles, making them ideal for pre-treatment processes. Both types are widely used in water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring efficient separation and purification.</p></p>
