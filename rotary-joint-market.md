<p><strong>The Rotary Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Circuit Rotary Joint</li><li>Double Circuit Rotary Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary joint market is categorized into two main types: single circuit and double circuit rotary joints. Single circuit rotary joints allow the passage of a single fluid, typically for applications requiring simple transmission of liquids or gases. In contrast, double circuit rotary joints handle two separate fluid circuits simultaneously, often used in more complex systems where different fluids need to be managed. Both types are essential in various industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and energy, enabling efficient fluid transfer in rotating equipment.</p></p>
