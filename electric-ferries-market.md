<p><p>The electric ferries market comprises two primary types: pure electric ferries and hybrid ferries. Pure electric ferries operate entirely on battery power, offering zero emissions and enhanced energy efficiency for short to medium distances. In contrast, hybrid ferries combine traditional fuel engines with electric propulsion, allowing for greater operational flexibility and reduced emissions. Both types aim to improve environmental sustainability in maritime transport, catering to the growing demand for cleaner, more efficient alternatives in passenger and freight transportation.</p></p>
