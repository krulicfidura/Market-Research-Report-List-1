<p><strong>The Connector Socket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PLCC Socket</li><li>DIP Socket</li><li>IC Socket</li><li>LGA Socket</li><li>Transistor Socket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The connector socket market encompasses various types, including PLCC sockets, DIP sockets, IC sockets, LGA sockets, and transistor sockets. PLCC (Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier) sockets support surface-mounted chips, while DIP (Dual In-line Package) sockets accommodate dual in-line package components. IC sockets are versatile for integrated circuits, allowing easy replacement. LGA (Land Grid Array) sockets provide a stable connection for high-performance CPUs, and transistor sockets facilitate the use of transistors in circuit designs. Each type serves specific applications in electronics.</p></p>
