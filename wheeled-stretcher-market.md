<p><p>The wheeled stretcher market comprises three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual stretchers require human effort for adjustments and movement, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Electric stretchers provide automated features for elevation and maneuverability, enhancing patient comfort and ease of use for medical staff. Hydraulic stretchers utilize a hydraulic system for smooth height adjustments and stability, making them ideal for transferring patients safely. Each type caters to different needs in emergency services and healthcare settings, prioritizing efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
