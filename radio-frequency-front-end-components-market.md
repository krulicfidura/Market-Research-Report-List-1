Power Amplifiers (PA) enhance signal strength for transmission, while RF Switches manage signal routing between paths. RF Filters eliminate unwanted frequencies, ensuring cleaner signals, and Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA) boost weak signals for improved reception. Other components may include duplexers and mixers, which play supporting roles in signal processing. Together, these components are crucial for optimizing wireless communication performance across various applications.</p></p>
