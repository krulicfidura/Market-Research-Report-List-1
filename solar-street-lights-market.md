<p><p>The solar street lights market comprises two main types: standalone and grid-connected. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, utilizing solar panels, batteries, and LED fixtures to provide off-grid lighting solutions. They are ideal for remote areas without electrical infrastructure. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights also harness solar energy but are integrated into the electrical grid, allowing for reduced energy costs and reliance on traditional power sources. Both types contribute to sustainable urban lighting and energy efficiency.</p></p>
