<p><strong>The Resistance Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quarter-bridge</li><li>Half-bridge</li><li>Full-bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance bridge market types refer to configurations for measuring resistance using a network of resistors. </p><p>- **Quarter-bridge** uses one active strain gauge and three fixed resistors, ideal for specific applications with minimal cost. </p><p>- **Half-bridge** incorporates two strain gauges and two fixed resistors, enhancing sensitivity and temperature compensation. </p><p>- **Full-bridge** employs four strain gauges, offering maximum accuracy and balanced measurements, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Each configuration caters to varying precision and complexity needs in resistance measurement applications.</p></p>
