<p><p>The electric propulsion satellites market encompasses several technologies, including Hall Effect Thrusters (HET), Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT), and others. HETs utilize magnetic fields to accelerate ions, offering high efficiency for long-duration missions. PPTs generate thrust through pulsed plasma bursts, providing a simpler design and lower power requirements. Other technologies include ion thrusters and electromagnetic thrusters, each catering to specific mission profiles and operational needs. Collectively, these systems enhance satellite maneuverability, extend operational life, and reduce fuel consumption.</p></p>
