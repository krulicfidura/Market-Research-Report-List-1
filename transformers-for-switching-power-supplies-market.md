<p><p>Transformers in switching power supplies are categorized into two market types: single-excited and double-excited. Single-excited transformers utilize a single power source, often providing simpler designs and cost-effective solutions for low-power applications. In contrast, double-excited transformers are supplied by two power sources, enhancing efficiency and power handling capabilities, making them suitable for higher power applications. Each type caters to different needs, balancing factors such as complexity, cost, and performance based on the specific requirements of various electronic devices.</p></p>
