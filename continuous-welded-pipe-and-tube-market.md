<p><p>The Continuous Welded Pipe and Tube market features two main types: Straight Seam Welded Pipe and Helical Welded Pipe. Straight Seam Welded Pipe is produced by welding flat metal strips edge-to-edge, creating a strong and consistent tube ideal for various applications. In contrast, Helical Welded Pipe employs a spiral welding technique, allowing for larger diameters and enhanced strength, making it suitable for high-pressure and structural applications. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and transportation.</p></p>
