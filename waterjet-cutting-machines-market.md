<p><ul><li>PressurizedWaterWaterjetCuttingMachines</li><li>AbrasiveMixtureWaterjetCuttingMachines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutting machines are categorized into two main types: pressurized water waterjet cutting machines and abrasive mixture waterjet cutting machines. Pressurized water machines utilize high-pressure water jets to cut through materials, ideal for softer substances like rubber or foam. In contrast, abrasive mixture waterjet cutting machines incorporate abrasive materials into the water stream, enabling them to cut through tougher materials such as metal, glass, and stone. Each type serves distinct applications based on the material properties and cutting requirements.</p></p>
