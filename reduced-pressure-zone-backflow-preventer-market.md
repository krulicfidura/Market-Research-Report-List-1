<p><p>A Reduced Pressure Zone Backflow Preventer (RPZ) is a type of device designed to prevent the reverse flow of contaminated water into the potable water supply. It consists of two independently acting check valves and a pressurized chamber that maintains a lower pressure than the supply side, thus ensuring safe and clean water delivery. RPZ devices are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings where the risk of contamination is higher.</p><p>The market for Reduced Pressure Zone Backflow Preventers is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing regulatory requirements for water safety and public health initiatives. Rising awareness about the importance of maintaining clean water supplies in urban areas, alongside the expansion of industrial applications, contributes to this demand.
