<p><p>The automatic banknote sorter market is categorized into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic sorters. Fully automatic banknote sorters operate independently, efficiently processing and sorting large volumes of currency with minimal human intervention. They are ideal for high-traffic environments like banks and casinos. In contrast, semi-automatic sorters require some manual input, allowing operators to oversee operations while still streamlining the sorting process. This type is suitable for smaller businesses or locales with less frequent cash handling needs.</p></p>
