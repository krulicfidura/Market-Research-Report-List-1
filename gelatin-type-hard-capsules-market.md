<p><strong>The Gelatin Type Hard Capsules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gelatin type hard capsules are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their composition and properties. Type I capsules are made from hydrolyzed gelatin, offering increased barrier properties and superior stability against moisture, making them ideal for sensitive formulations. Type II capsules, on the other hand, are made from regular gelatin, which allows for greater flexibility in design and cost-effectiveness. Both types serve various applications in pharmaceuticals and nutraceuticals, providing effective delivery methods for active ingredients.</p></p>
