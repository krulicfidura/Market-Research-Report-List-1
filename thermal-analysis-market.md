<p><p>The thermal analysis market comprises various types of analyzers used to study material properties under temperature changes. Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) measures heat flow variations to analyze thermal transitions. Thermogravimetric analyzers (TGA) assess weight changes related to temperature to investigate thermal stability. Simultaneous Thermal Analyzers (STA) combine DSC and TGA functionalities. Differential Thermal Analyzers (DTA) evaluate temperature differences during heat changes, while Thermomechanical Analyzers (TMA) examine dimensional changes. Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers (DMA) assess mechanical properties with temperature, and other market segments include specialty analyzers catering to niche applications.</p></p>
