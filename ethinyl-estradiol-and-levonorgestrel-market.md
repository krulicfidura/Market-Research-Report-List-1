The demand for these combination products has been driven by increasing awareness of reproductive health and family planning, as well as advancements in pharmaceutical formulations.</p><p>The Ethinyl Estradiol and Levonorgestrel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period.
