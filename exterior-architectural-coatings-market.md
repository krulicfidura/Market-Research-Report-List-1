<p><strong>The Exterior Architectural Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Water-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exterior architectural coatings market primarily comprises two types: solvent-borne and water-borne coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents as their primary carrier, offering durability and strong adhesion, making them suitable for harsh environments. However, they often emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. In contrast, water-borne coatings use water as the main solvent, promoting lower VOC emissions and being more environmentally friendly. They provide good performance and ease of application, making them increasingly popular in sustainable building practices.</p></p>
