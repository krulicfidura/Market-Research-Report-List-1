<p><p>Contact and convective dryers are essential equipment used in various industries for drying materials. Contact dryers, such as paddle dryers and drum dryers, utilize direct contact with a heating surface to transfer heat to the material, facilitating moisture removal. Convective dryers, on the other hand, rely on hot air or gas flow to evaporate moisture, making them suitable for bulk drying applications.</p><p>The Contact and Convective Dryers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand across sectors like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.
