<p><p>The ultrasonic anemometer market is primarily divided into two types: two-dimensional (2D) and three-dimensional (3D) sonic anemometers. Two-dimensional sonic anemometers measure wind speed and direction in a horizontal plane, making them suitable for applications like meteorology and environmental monitoring. In contrast, three-dimensional sonic anemometers capture wind velocity in all three dimensions, providing more comprehensive data for applications including research, aviation, and energy production. The choice between the two depends on the required accuracy and application context.</p></p>
