<p><p>The Arteriosclerosis Testers Market comprises various types, primarily focused on assessing arterial health. Pulse Wave Velocity (PWV) measures the speed of blood flow through arteries, indicating arterial stiffness, a key marker of cardiovascular risk. Central-Femoral PWV (CF-PWV) specifically evaluates the arterial elasticity between the central and peripheral circulatory systems, offering a precise analysis of cardiovascular health. Both testing types are crucial for early detection of arteriosclerosis, aiding in preventative healthcare measures and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
