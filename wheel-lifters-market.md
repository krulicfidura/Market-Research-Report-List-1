<p><p>The wheel lifters market can be categorized into three types based on their power source: hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic wheel lifters utilize fluid pressure for lifting, offering high lifting capacity and stability. Electric wheel lifters operate through electric motors, providing ease of use and mobility for various tasks. Pneumatic wheel lifters rely on compressed air for operation, known for their lightweight design and quick lifting action. Each type serves specific needs in automotive and industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and user convenience.</p></p>
