<p><ul><li>Uniform Fiber Bragg Grating</li><li>Non Uniform Fiber Bragg Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBG) market is typically divided into two main types: Uniform Fiber Bragg Gratings and Non-Uniform Fiber Bragg Gratings. Uniform FBGs exhibit consistent periodic refractive index modulation, making them ideal for applications requiring precise wavelength filtering and sensing. In contrast, Non-Uniform FBGs feature varied periodicity, allowing for enhanced capabilities in dynamic environments and complex signal processing. Both types serve essential roles in telecommunications, structural health monitoring, and sensor applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
