<p><p>Modular bridges come in various types, each suited to specific engineering needs and applications. Beam-type bridges utilize horizontal beams for support, providing straightforward installation and cost-effectiveness. Arch-type bridges employ curved structures to distribute weight efficiently, enhancing aesthetic appeal and strength. Suspension bridges feature cables and towers, offering long spans ideal for challenging terrains but requiring more complex assembly. Other types include cantilever and truss bridges, catering to specialized requirements and environments, showcasing versatility in modular bridge solutions.</p></p>
