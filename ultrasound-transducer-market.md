<p><ul><li>Linear Transducer</li><li>Pencil Transducer</li><li>Surface Shape Transducer</li><li>Concave Transducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound transducer market encompasses various types tailored for specific imaging applications. Linear transducers deliver high-resolution images for vascular and musculoskeletal assessments due to their flat shape. Pencil transducers, characterized by their small size, are ideal for focused imaging, commonly used in cardiac evaluations. Surface shape transducers feature curved surfaces to enhance image quality in abdominal scans, while concave transducers provide wider field views, making them suitable for obstetric and gynecological applications. Each type serves unique diagnostic needs.</p></p>
