<p><strong>The Dosimetry Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TLD</li><li>OSL</li><li>RPL</li><li>Active Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dosimetry equipment market comprises various types used for measuring radiation exposure. Thermoluminescent dosimeters (TLD) rely on heat to release stored energy as light, indicating radiation levels. Optically stimulated luminescence (OSL) dosimeters use light stimulation to measure accumulated dose. Radiophotoluminescent (RPL) dosimeters utilize specific materials that emit light when exposed to radiation. Active dosimeters, often electronic, provide real-time monitoring with continuous data readouts. Each type serves distinct applications in radiation safety and monitoring across industries.</p></p>
