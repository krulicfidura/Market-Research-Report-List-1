<p><p>The stem cell antibody market is categorized into primary and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are designed to bind directly to specific stem cell antigens, playing a crucial role in identifying and isolating stem cells for research and therapeutic applications. Secondary antibodies, on the other hand, bind to primary antibodies and are typically conjugated with labels for visualization or detection. Together, they enhance the sensitivity and specificity of experiments, facilitating advancements in stem cell research and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
