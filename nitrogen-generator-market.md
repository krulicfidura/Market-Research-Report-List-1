<p><strong>The Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generator</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generator market comprises two main types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) nitrogen generators and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA generators utilize a two-step process involving adsorption and desorption to separate nitrogen from air, delivering high-purity nitrogen suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, membrane nitrogen generators employ selective permeation through polymer membranes to separate nitrogen, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution for lower purity requirements. Both technologies cater to diverse industries including pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
