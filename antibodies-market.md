<p><strong>The Antibodies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monoclonal antibodies</li><li>Polyclonal antibodies</li><li>Antibody drug conjugates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antibodies market comprises three main types: monoclonal antibodies, polyclonal antibodies, and antibody-drug conjugates (ADCs). Monoclonal antibodies are identical antibodies produced from a single clone, targeting specific antigens for therapeutic use. Polyclonal antibodies are a mixture from various immune cells, offering a broader response. Antibody-drug conjugates combine antibodies with cytotoxic drugs, delivering targeted therapy directly to cancer cells, minimizing damage to healthy tissues. Together, these types address various medical conditions, including cancer, autoimmune diseases, and infections.</p></p>
