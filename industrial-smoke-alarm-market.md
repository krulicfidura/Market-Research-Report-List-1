<p><p>The industrial smoke alarm market includes three primary types: Photoelectric smoke alarms, which utilize a light sensor to detect smoke particles and are effective for slow-burning fires; Ionization smoke alarms, which use radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires and respond more quickly to high flames; and Dual Sensor smoke alarms, which combine both technologies to offer comprehensive coverage for various fire scenarios. Each type serves distinct environments, ensuring enhanced safety and compliance in industrial settings.</p></p>
