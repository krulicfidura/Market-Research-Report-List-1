Centrifugal machines utilize high-speed rotation to mix ingredients and facilitate uniform baking, often improving texture and rise. Other types encompass specialized equipment such as convection ovens and conveyor systems, designed for various baking processes, catering to diverse product needs in the food production industry.</p></p>
